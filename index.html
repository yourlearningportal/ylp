Your Learning Portal was created by a GOSH staff member for us to all share the knowledge and skills that we have via quick video tutorials. The aim is for staff members to create their own material for those with a smaller knowledge-base in certain programmes who can then learn quick tips that can save them a lot of time.

How to record your own video

If you would like to submit some tips for other staff members, you can use the video below to learn how you can screen record. You can also submit any documents you have created that you feel may be helpful. Once you have created your video or document, please send it to michael.speakman@gosh.nhs.uk



Videos you create are advised to be under 4 minutes.

Your Learning Portal is a free resource that has been created to try and assist other staff members, I would welcome any feedback which can be sent to michael.speakman@gosh.nhs.uk
